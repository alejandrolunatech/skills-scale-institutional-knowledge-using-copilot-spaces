# OctoAcme — Risk Owner Responsibilities Checklist

Use this checklist to guide the Risk Owner's ongoing responsibilities throughout the project lifecycle. Refer to `octoacme-risks-and-communication.md` for the Risk Register format and escalation paths.

---

## At Project Kickoff

- [ ] Review initial Risk Register with the Project Manager
- [ ] Confirm each identified risk has an Owner, Impact rating, Likelihood rating, and Mitigation plan
- [ ] Ensure risk review cadence is scheduled (recommended: weekly)

## Weekly Risk Review

- [ ] Update status of each active risk in the Risk Register
- [ ] Verify mitigation actions are progressing on schedule
- [ ] Identify any new risks raised since the last review
- [ ] Escalate risks that have increased in likelihood or impact to the Project Manager
- [ ] Share risk summary in the weekly status report

## Pre-Release Risk Review

- [ ] Confirm no unmitigated High-impact risks remain open
- [ ] Communicate release-blocking risks to the Release Manager
- [ ] Document final risk status in the release readiness checklist

## Risk Escalation Triggers

Escalate immediately when:
- A risk's likelihood or impact increases to High
- A mitigation action is overdue by more than one sprint
- A risk materializes and becomes an active incident

## Risk Register Hygiene

- [ ] Close risks that have been fully mitigated with a resolution note
- [ ] Archive resolved risks at the end of each milestone or release
- [ ] Capture lessons learned from materialized risks in the retrospective
